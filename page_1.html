π (pi)

π is an irrational number used in mathematics to represent the rario of a circle's circumference to its diameter. At A Level, π appears in several key formulae including: the area of a sector, the volume of a solid of revolution about the x or y axis, the representation of complex numbers in exponential form and many geometric volume and area formulae.

The most famous use of π in mathematical formula outside of those for the area and circumference of circles is in the equation e^iπ + 1 = 0.
